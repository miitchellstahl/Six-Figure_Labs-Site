Six Figure Roadmap

Phase 1

UX Research

In order for your website to get as much engagement as possible, our team studies, disects, and analyzes your business. We create a gameplan, and a whole boatload of notes. Our specialists strategically pick the best elements and content, and send everything to the designers.

Wireframe Prototype

We use all the data from the UX findings to mockup your site, each page is designed to meet the needs of the client. We start by creating a color scheme, choosing typography, and collecting all content. We remain in close contact with you every step of the way, no page is finished until we receive your approval.

Phase 3

Development

Our development team converts every page design to a responsive and fast website, creating an identical copy of the mockup. Our tech stack includes HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and much more. Every project is thoroughly tested on a variety of devices for bugs before launch.

Launch

Once the site is cleared of all bugs, we archive the code in our database and host it. All domain configurations and backend solutions are finalized.

Six Figure Questions

How long does a website take to create?

Each and every project is unique and requires a different amount of time to complete. After careful analysis of your company, we figure out exactly what is needed and formulate a project brief. Our briefs contain a comprehensive timeline and blueprint, so you can easily follow us every step of the way. Although every project is unique, our turnaround time is usually between two and four weeks.

How will I add future content?

Our goal is to make your life as easy and efficient as possible, that’s why we handle all the changes. Once the website is completed, any edit, feature, and styling change will be made by us. We know changes often need to be taken into effect immediately. Our team gets instant text alerts whenever a change is requested, and we prioritize your modification in order to make it live as fast as possible.

Why use Six Figure Labs over a no code solution

We know there are many no code web creators out there, and choosing Six Figure Labs may not be the most obvious decision. Our designers and developers are experts in the field, no code solutions are not. We believe websites are not just for putting your company on the internet, they are powerful marketing tools that need a strategy behind them. Our team not only creates the site, but we develop that strategy.

How will a custom design benefit me?

There is no one size fits all design for your company. Every website serves a different purpose and therefore needs a unique design and strategy plan to best suit those needs. From buttons to images, every element is there for a reason, whether that be to get more clients for a law firm, or to sell chocolate bars. Only a custom design with an award winning UX strategy can truly drive sales and engagement.

How much does a website cost?

We charge based on the length and complexity of the project. Unlike many web development agencies that have a minimum price, we accept any project. The average rate for a project is between $500 and $5000, depending on what’s needed. We will always give you the best price possible. We offer many payment options!

Do I need to provide all the content?

No, you do not need to provide all the content. However, that will speed up the process. We will provide you with digital graphics free of charge, this includes icons, isometric images, and any other graphic design you need. Once we begin the design process, we’ll send you a list of everything we need. If you don’t have the material, we’ll work with you to create it, whether that be content, images, or graphics.
